Tomato Pie

Home

Ingredients

  • pie crust
  • mayonnaise
  • mustard
  • tomatoes
  • cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Line a pie dish with the pie crust.
  3. In a bowl, mix mayonnaise and mustard together.
  4. Layer sliced tomatoes in the pie crust, seasoning each layer with salt and pepper.
  5. Spread the mayonnaise mixture over the tomatoes.
  6. Top with shredded cheese.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  8. Let cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
