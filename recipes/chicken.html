Chicken Recipe

Home

Description

This chicken recipe is a delightful blend of flavors and spices that will tantalize your taste buds. Perfectly cooked chicken pieces are marinated in a mixture of herbs and spices, then grilled to perfection. Served with a side of fresh vegetables or rice, this dish is sure to be a hit at any dinner table.

Ingredients

  • vinegar
  • mustard
  • boneless chicken
  • garlic
  • olive oil

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Marinate chicken for at least 2 hours.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until cooked through.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite sides.
